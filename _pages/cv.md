## 🔬 Research Experience

**Stroke Rehabilitation Exoskeletons** \
📅 *Jan 2026 – Present*

- **Key Contributions:** Participated in the mechanical structure design and physical assembly of the exoskeleton. Developed and deployed a deep learning-based gait prediction framework that uses neural networks to predict real-time kinematic states of the affected leg.

**Deep Learning-Based Unified Exoskeleton Control** \

📅 *Nov 2024 – Feb 2025*
