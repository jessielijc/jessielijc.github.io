I am an undergraduate student majoring in **Robotics Engineering** at [Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/en/), expected to graduate in 2027. 

My research lies at the intersection of **Deep Learning, Human-Robot Interaction, and Biomechanics**. I am currently working on deep learning-based gait prediction and unified control frameworks for stroke rehabilitation and fitness exoskeletons. Additionally, I am passionate about developing multimodal autonomous mobile manipulation systems using ROS 2.

Throughout my academic journey, I have maintained a GPA of 3.76/4.0 (3.98 in the latest semester) and received the Second Prize Scholarship twice. I am also highly involved in student leadership and volunteer work, serving as the Director of the Organization Department at SUSTech Red Cross and an AHA HeartSaver.

I am currently looking for research internships and prospective Ph.D./Master opportunities! Feel free to reach out via [email](mailto:jessielijc@outlook.com).
